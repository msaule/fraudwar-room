# LinkedIn Post Draft

I built FraudWar Room, an adaptive fraud simulation and investigation cockpit.

Most fraud demos stop at a static classifier. FraudWar Room generates a synthetic payment
network with benign users, merchants, fraud rings, graph evidence, alerts, cases, and
investigator capacity. Synthetic rings adapt after defensive friction, and defenses are
evaluated on fraud loss, false-positive harm, ring-level recall, adversarial half-life, and
investigator ROI.

The project includes a Python/FastAPI simulation engine, NetworkX graph features, detection
baselines, executive after-action reports, and a polished risk operations dashboard.
